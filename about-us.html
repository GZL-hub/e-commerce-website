Our Story

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with the highest quality and most stylish options available. We strive to offer a wide range of products that cater to different tastes and needs, from transitional style to more modern decor idea. We believe that furniture is not just a necessity, but a reflection of one's personality and style. That's why we take pride in our ability to innovate and design furnitures that not only meets our customers' needs, but also exceeds expecatations.

Our Founder

Octavia Böer's furniture was founded by John Böer's, the name Octavia came from his great-grandmother's name. His journey began when their family are in the carpentry business where John has found great interest in crafting wooden furniture.John started his furniture business in his hometown, Oslo, where John is able to design and manufacture furntiures. He ensured a wide variety of styles and designs were available, gaining a eestemed reputation for his high-quality and elegant product. His story serves as a testament to the power of passion, hard work, and a commitment to quality

Craftmanship / Materials

Our mission is to create furniture that not only serves a purpose but also tells a story, it began with a deep appreciation for the timeless beauty of wood/We are guided by our core values of quality, authenticity, and sustainability. Through partnerships and a supply chain infrastructure, we connect manufacturers to markets, enabling products to reach their destinations swiftly and reliably. From brick-and-mortar establishments e-commerce, we curate an extensive range of products, ensuring that we suit every customer's needs and provide good customer service
